Holes
- Every 20 seconds the intensity increases, which means more frequent hole spawns.

Color event
- Happens every 20 seconds.
- You get 5 seconds to be on the right lane to take your boost.

Boost/Dash
- Every time you take a boost your boost amount increases by 20.
- When you use boost, your boost amount decreases by time till it reaches 0, you can also stop your boost to save some for later.
- You are immune for holes.

Score
- Score increases by time.
- Taking a boost will get you +5000 score instantly.

Controls
- Keyboard: left arrow, right arrow and space for boost.
- Xbox 360 controller: left trigger, right trigger and A for boost.
